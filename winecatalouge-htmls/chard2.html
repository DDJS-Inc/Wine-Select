Name: Hirsch Estate From: Sonoma Coast, CA, USA Produced from 100 percent estate fruit, native yeasts propel fermentation, while the majority of aging takes place in French and Austrian oak. Savory dried herbs and green leaf notes build layers of complexity upon its tart fruit and floral core. The wines texture is round, its alcohol content does not register, and the finish does not linger but instead sets up camp for the long haul. Apple-crumble, lemon-rind and sugar-cookie aromas follow through to a full body with a dense center-palate and lots of rich fruit and phenolic tension. Honey and candied apples at the end. A rich, intense wine. Price: $$
